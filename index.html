About Sophia

We move, we breathe, we laugh.

Sophia's approach to yoga is rooted in joy, curiosity, and genuine care for every student who steps onto the mat. No perfection required - just presence, breath, and the willingness to show up.

Classes blend strength, flexibility, and mindfulness into something that feels less like exercise and more like coming home to yourself.

Inclusive
Yoga for every body, every ability, every age.
Joyful
We take the practice seriously, not ourselves.
Grounded
Real instruction. Real results. Real connection.
Community
You'll leave with more than a yoga practice.
